Cyber Attacks

As the world relies more on technoloy, it is important to know how to stay safe on the internet.

About This Project

This project serves to demonstrate the dangers of various cyber attacks and ways to avoid them. Each module will allow the user to experience a simulated internet interaction. Users will observe the various attacks scammers utilize to gain personal information and will learn how to avoid them.

Modules

Viruses
Computer viruses are malicious codes placed within documents or programs with the goal to alter the computer's function. A user can obtain a virus by opening a corrupted document or running a corrupted file. Click the image above to vist an example of how a person would obtain a virus.
Ransomware
Ransomware is a form of malware focused on obtaining the user's information or files and encrypting them. The attacker then demands payment from the user in exchange for returning access to the encrypted data. Click the image above to see a simulated ransomware website.
Phishing
Phishing is a cyber attack designed to trick a user into opening a malicious email. That email will then try to steal the user's data and information. Click the image above to experience an example of phishing.
Password Attacks
Password attacks are various methods used to guess a user's password and obtain access to their accounts. If the hacker is able to gain access to the user's account, their information may be compromised. Click the image above to experience the signs of a password attack.
